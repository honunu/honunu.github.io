Privacy Policy for Winter Hideout

Effective Date: April 2024

1. Introduction

Winter Hideout (“the App”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy informs you about our practices regarding any information we might access or store.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or usage information. Winter Hideout is designed as a fully offline experience:

  • ✅ The App functions entirely on your device.
  • ✅ No personal identifiers (like name, email, or device ID) are collected.
  • ✅ No usage analytics, diagnostics, or crash reports are gathered.
  • ✅ No third-party analytics or advertising frameworks are integrated.
  • ✅ Your preferences (like scene selection) are stored locally on your device only and are never sent to us.

3. Third-Party Services

The App does not utilize any third-party services, SDKs, or libraries that collect data.

4. Changes to This Policy

If our data practices change in the future, we will:

  • Update this Privacy Policy.
  • Update the privacy labels on the App Store.
  • Obtain your explicit consent within the App where required.
